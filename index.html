How it started...

#OnceUponAColemanin a dim and empty pace a guy longed for at least one sell of his teacakes. “But why am I here”, he thought as he scurried to his car in disbelief. Until he caught a glimpse of a single soul enter into the MOSAIC. “It’s hope for me yet”, thought the guy as he gathered himself to reenter the place and claim his spot at the bar once again. He waited and waited, watching each person walk in and gravitate to one side. Until the door opened and in walked a vibrant, young, TIRED, BIG HAIR girl who was dragged out for the night in a work t-shirt and tennis shoes. The guy watched as she walked around the room and made her way to the bar. He was so dazzled by her beauty that all he could say when came close enough in his range was… “We need to talk!” And from there...

the rest is history...

Venue

Houston’s newest & finest wedding & special event venue! Sitting on 32 acres with the best waterfront view in town, White Oaks on the Bayou is located in Simonton, just a few miles West of Katy & Fulshear. It is the perfect canvas to host any memorable event!

White Oaks on the Bayou is perfect for all of life’s special occasions. Whether you are looking to host a wedding, ceremony, rehearsal dinner, corporate event, birthday, gala, bar-mitzvah, quinceanera, bridal shower, baby shower, family reunion, private meeting, luncheon or anniversary party…our venue adds a certain touch of class you won’t find anywhere else.
